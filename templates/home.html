Frequently Asked Questions about Arts

What are the different types of visual arts?
Visual arts include painting, drawing, sculpture, photography, printmaking, and digital art. Each type has different styles and mediums, making visual arts a vast category.
What are performing arts?
Performing arts involve disciplines like theater, dance, music, and opera. These forms of art are typically performed live and focus on artistic expression through movement, acting, and music.
How is digital art different from traditional art?
Digital art is created using digital tools and software, whereas traditional art uses physical materials like paint, canvas, and paper. Digital art allows for more flexibility in editing and production.
